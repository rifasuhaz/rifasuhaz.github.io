01 — about

Hey! I'm a researcher based in Frankfurt working in computational seismology. My ongoing PhD focuses on seismic anisotropy and the development of 3D shear-wave velocity models using data from the AdriaArray project.

Beyond my research, I'm passionate about data science, machine learning, and web development. I enjoy traveling as well :)

I did my Bachelor-Master studies at IISER Pune, and currently pursuing PhD at Goethe University, Germany.
